Hello! My name is Sunil Kumar.

I started Tranquility Treks in 1995. I was born in Nainital and have lived here all of my life. I specialize in customized itineraries to suit independent travellers, from local day hikes around Nainital to overnight homestays with traditional Indian families. There is no better way to experience the flavor of the Kumaon region than to be the guest of a traditional local family.

No matter what type of trekking experience you decide upon, you will enjoy the natural beauty and tranquility of the Kumaon countryside, with all its diverse flora and fauna, and breath-taking views of the Himalayas.
